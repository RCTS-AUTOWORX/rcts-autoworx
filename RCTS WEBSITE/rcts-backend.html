QuickBooks Integration Notes

QuickBooks 2019 Desktop can be connected to this dashboard via Zapier. When a lead is marked as Booked in your leads table, Zapier can automatically:

  • ✅  Create a new customer record in QuickBooks
  • ✅  Generate a draft invoice with vehicle & service details
  • ✅  Assign the invoice to the correct job category
  • ⬜  Set up Zapier connection — Open Zapier →
