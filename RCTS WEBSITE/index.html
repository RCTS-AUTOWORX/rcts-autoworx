Who We Are
HKS' OLDEST ACTIVE DEALER
IN CANADA

RCTS specializes in complete automotive repairs on all makes and models. This includes engine rebuilding, clutch replacements, A/C servicing, transmission and differential repairs, fluid changes and flushes. We also perform pre-purchase, safety, and insurance inspections.

Over the years RCTS Autoworx has worked with factory race shops, contracts and sponsorships including TTE-Toyota Team Europe-TMG, TMME Toyota Europe, TRD Japan, HKS USA, HKS Co., Ltd Japan and many more.

Staffed with seasoned professionals, the RCTS team prides themselves on honesty, quality, knowledge and commitment. All technicians, management and technical personnel are fully licensed and trained to top industry standards — and most importantly, they're motorsports enthusiasts.
